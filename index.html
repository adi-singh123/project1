What is E-Waste ?

E-waste, short for electronic waste, refers to discarded electrical or electronic devices. This category of waste includes a wide range of items, such as computers, smartphones, tablets, televisions, refrigerators, washing machines, and other consumer electronics. E-waste also encompasses electronic components, cables, batteries, and other accessories associated with these devices.
